Learning Beyond Textbooks

We believe that learning should go beyond textbooks — it should spark imagination, innovation, and independence.

At INFIVITY, we teach students aged 6–18 the skills that define the future: Coding, Robotics, AI, Arts, and Public Speaking — all through fun, practical, and project-based learning.

Our approach combines technology, creativity, and communication to prepare students for real-world challenges from an early age.

Our Mission

To make every child a creator, not just a consumer of technology — helping them discover their interests, build skills, and develop confidence to lead in a digital world.

Our Vision

To build a generation of thinkers, innovators, and changemakers who will drive Nepal — and the world — toward a more creative and tech-empowered future.
